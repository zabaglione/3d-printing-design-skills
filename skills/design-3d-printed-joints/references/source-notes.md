## Synthesis principles

- Treat the videos as a catalog of mechanisms, not a table of guaranteed dimensions.
- Preserve the general function of each pattern while changing geometry for process, load, material, and assembly direction.
- Separate locating, load transfer, retention, and hard-stop functions.
- Calibrate fits with representative coupons and record the qualified process.
- Prefer failure-mode testing over visual confidence.
- Treat data sheets and creator results as starting evidence; qualify the exact hardware, material condition, geometry, orientation, and installation method.
